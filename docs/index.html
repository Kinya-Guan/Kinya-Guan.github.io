About

I am a PhD student (D1) in the Department of Physics at the University of Tokyo and a member of the Katsura Group.

I am also affiliated with the Forefront Physics and Mathematics Program to Drive Transformation (FoPM).

My research interests lie in condensed matter physics, statistical mechanics, and mathematical physics. I am currently working on quantum many-body systems and their dynamics.
